About Us

MeloBox is a unique platform designed to help you achieve the ultimate listening experience. We help our users find new music, artists, and genres, as well as reconnect them with music of their past.

Stay up to date with the latest tracks in your country with our search-by-location feature, find artists similar to your favorites using our search-by-name feature, and discover if your favorite artist will be performing near you.

Our search engines are designed to help you find the music best suited to your tastes.

We have utilized APIs from last.fm and Bandsintown, and graphics from logomakr.com to improve our quality of our website and broaden our databases. The pictures used in the backgrounds are located on unsplash.com and come from various sources.

This website was created during the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program in 2018.
